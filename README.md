# 📊 AI Sales Pipeline Dashboard — Power BI

> Power BI dashboard analyzing 250 AI-scored B2B leads — built as a portfolio project to demonstrate business analytics, data visualization, and AI-driven insights.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Overview

This dashboard visualizes the output of the [AI Sales Pipeline Automation](https://github.com/erwananglais-creator/ai-sales-pipeline-automation) project — 250 B2B leads scored by GPT-4o-mini and analyzed across multiple business dimensions.

**3 KPI Cards at a glance:**
- 📊 **250** total leads processed
- 🔥 **66** Hot leads identified (26.4%)
- ⭐ **47.62** average AI score across all leads

---

## 🔍 Visuals & Insights

| Visual | Insight |
|--------|---------|
| **Hot / Warm / Cold Donut** | 37.2% Cold, 36.4% Hot, 26.4% Warm |
| **Avg AI Score by Sector** | LegalTech & HealthTech lead in score quality |
| **Leads by Source** | LinkedIn Outbound generates the most leads |
| **Avg AI Score by Country** | Belgium and Spain top-performing markets |
| **Top Hot Leads Table** | Filtered view of highest-scored leads with company & job title |
